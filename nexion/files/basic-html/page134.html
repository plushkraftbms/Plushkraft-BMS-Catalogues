                                    TECHNICAL INFOThough the main focus of customers is on aesthetics, physical features and chemical properties of slabs and tiles are very important for the eventual result of any surface installation and, in the end, for customer%u2019s satisfaction.Physical features are important for the ease and quality of installation and for some aspects of durability, in particular with slabs and large size tiles, that cannot be installed properly if their geometry is not perfect and that might break if not flexible enough (contrary to conventional belief, a slab is more robust if flexible rather than rigid). The key physical features are:%u2014 The sharpness and regularity of geometrical dimensions, as well as the overall surface quality (for whose assessment Nexion has devised a proprietary set of procedures %u2013 described in the next section %u2013 that goes well beyond ISO norms) %u2014 The physical properties that affect installation and durability.Chemical properties are important for maintenance and other aspects of surface durability, as they determine resistance to stain and acids, as well as cleanability.Usage-Related Performance PropertiesUsage-related features are important for maintenance and other aspects of surface and body durability, as they determine the surface performance such as its resistance to traffic, stains and acids, as well as cleanability and slip resistance.Dimension and surface characteristicsGeometrical characteristics of products are important for the ease and quality of installation:%u2014 Precise dimensions are important in particular for large size slabs (above 600x1200 mm), that cannot be installed properly if their geometry is not perfect%u2014 Flatness is important in particular for small sizes (below or equal 600x1200 mm) as high quality slabs flex and naturally adjust to the floor (a perfectly flat floor can be easily installed following Nexion Installation Procedure for large slabs)Intrinsic Physical CharacteristicsPhysical properties of slabs are important for the durability of the surface, both indoor and outdoor (floors and fa%u00e7ades):%u2014 Very low water absorption is key to avoid penetration of humidity and chemicals that may create stains, deteriorate the surface or cause breakages of the body%u2014 High breaking strength and modulus of rupture are important to sustain weight in flooring applications and to resist high wind load pressure in fa%u00e7ades%u2014 The superior physical performance of slabs using high quality body and proper syntherization is a key advantage compared to natural stone.NEXION%u2019S FOCUS ON QUALITYParameters of physical & chemical qualityBreaking strength and module of rupture indicate the tiles%u2019 resistance to flexion and loads. High values mean that slabs and tiles will not break under weight, with requirements depending on the application (residential, commercial, industrial %u2026).The high breaking strength of Nexion%u2019s slabs and large size tiles guarantee safe and long lasting installation.Frost resistance, coefficient of linear thermal expansion consider the temperature variables that the ceramic material can be subjected. These tests consider extreme hot-cold environmental situations that may be present in outdoor installation.Thanks to thermal characteristics, Nexion slabs and tiles can be safely used in exterior.The number following the letter R - from 9 to 13 %u2013 represents the anti-slip property of the tile.These are the characteristics of the 5 slipperiness classes, according to the measurement method foreseen by DIN 51130:%u2014 R9 normal adhesion%u2014 R10 medium adhesion.%u2014 R11 high adhesion.%u2014 R12 strong adhesion.DIN 51097 provides for the use of the coefficients %u201cA%u201d, %u201cB%u201d and %u201cC%u201d.%u2014 A medium adhesion, these are the floors that we would normally find in areas where you can access with bare feet.%u2014 B high adhesion, tiles used to cover showers or swimming pool edges%u2014 C strong adhesion.
