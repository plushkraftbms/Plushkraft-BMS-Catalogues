                                    ApplicationThese cables are used as measuring and control cables in tool machinery, plant installation, power stations and in data equipment. The braided screen offers best possible protection against mechanical damage. The galvanised coating on the steel wire braiding not only helps protect against corrosion, but also notably improves the soldering performance.
