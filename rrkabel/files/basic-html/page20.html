                                    CCTV CAMERA CABLEApplicationThese cables are specifically designed to transmit complete video frequency with minimum distortion or attenuation for security and surveillance. This cable is offered in two variants viz., 4+1 and 3+1 CCTV Camera cable.PropertiesCCTV cables are designed to optimize the quality of video signals. The dense tin coated copper screen ensures complete elimination of EMI/RFI from video signals and also provides reduced DC resistance ground path.
